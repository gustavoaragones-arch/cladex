- How It Works
- Pricing
- About

No clutter. No blog yet.

## 2. Hero Section

- **Headline:** Close confidently. Keep your equity.
- **Subheadline:** Structured transaction workflow software for buyers and sellers.
- **Primary CTA:** Start Your Transaction
- **Secondary CTA:** See How It Works

## 3. Problem Framing Section

- **Title:** The friction is not savings. It is uncertainty.
- **Bullets:** Large commission costs, Complex documentation, Deadline pressure, Fear of mistakes
- **Tone:** Measured. Data-informed. Not aggressive.

## 4. Solution Section

- **Title:** Structured execution.
- **Four blocks:**
  - Guided Stage Progression
  - Offer Risk Comparison
  - Risk Radar Monitoring
  - Professional Review Integration
- Each block: Short paragraph + calm iconography.

## 5. How It Works (5 Steps)

1. Create your transaction
2. Follow structured stages
3. Compare offers with risk scoring
4. Coordinate inspections and documentation
5. Close deliberately

## 6. Trust Section

- Regulatory insulation.
